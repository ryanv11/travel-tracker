DECISION: Option 1 — patch-package + migrate-only workflow

Rationale is recorded in ADL-15 (architecture-decisions-log.md). Summary:

  - Drizzle ORM (query layer) is working correctly. The bugs are exclusively
    in drizzle-kit's SQLite diff engine.

  - Two tracked migrations already exist. Switching to raw SQL now would
    orphan that history and add permanent maintenance burden (Option 2).

  - Option 3 (new ORM stack) is too disruptive pre-production. Option 4
    (wait upstream) leaves the patched binary fragile indefinitely.

  - patch-package is the right tool here: the patch is small (103 lines),
    reviewed, committed to version control, and fully auditable.
